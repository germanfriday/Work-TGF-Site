Common Adaptive Equipment

Provider Types

A - Assessment Centers: Driver assessment centers help select adaptive equipment for drivers and passengers. They will write "prescriptions" for adaptive equipment and offer adaptive equipment training.

D - Ford Lincoln Mercury Dealers

I - Installers: Installers understand a customer's adaptive equipment needs and the kind of vehicle in which it will be installed. A written estimate should be obtained before the job begins.

S - State Agencies: Agencies provide information and assistance to drivers with physical disabilities.
