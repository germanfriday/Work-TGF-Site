Vehicle Financing


Make sure you understand the final cost of your vehicle, before making your purchase. Obtain quotes from your Ford or Lincoln Mercury dealer concerning the vehicle sale price and your mobility modifier concerning the adaptive equipment and installation costs. Once you have a final cost, your Ford or Lincoln Mercury dealer will provide you with all of your financing options, such as Ford Credit Mobility Financing.

Ford Credit Financial – Mobility Financing

Ford Credit offers flexible finance terms for persons with physical disabilities and their families. In addition, they will finance both the vehicle and the adaptive equipment for up to 108 months for qualified customers enrolled in the Ford Mobility Motoring Program. The maximum terms available are determined by the total purchase price of the modified vehicle. Ford Credit finances the adaptive equipment, the conversion cost and the base vehicle as a single transaction. Further information is available at www.fordcredit.com/mobilityfinancing. The following table shows the finance terms available to qualifying customers under the plan. The examples of the amounts financed include the cost of the vehicle, plus any adaptive equipment.

Finance terms available to qualifying customers
Total Purchase Price Maximum Term (months)
$25,000 - $30,000 84
$30,001 - $35,000 96
$35,001 - $40,000 102
$40,001 and above 108

Peace of Mind

Ford Credit’s Peace of Mind allows customers 62 years of age and older to lease or finance a new vehicle without worrying that they’re creating a future financial burden for their families. For details, visit www.fordcredit.com and look for “Peace of Mind” under the “Finance a Vehicle” tab.

Additional financing information is available in the Ford Mobility Resource Guide, which lists State Agencies that may be able to provide additional assistance to drivers or passengers with disabilities.
