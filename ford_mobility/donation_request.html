Sponsorship Requests

Welcome to the Ford Mobility Motoring Program Donation Request Form. This form allows you to request a donation or sponsorship for your organization or organization’s event. Please note that once your request has been submitted, our staff will review your request for support and notify you as to its status within approximately 4 weeks of the submission date.

Completing this form does not guarantee that your request will be approved by the Ford Mobility Motoring Program. The Ford Mobility Motoring Program does not support the donation of vehicles.

Fields marked with red asterisk (*) are mandatory.
