Your Vehicle Selection Team

Choosing the right vehicle for your needs is a team effort. The team is composed of you, your evaluator, your Ford or Lincoln Mercury dealer and your qualified Mobility Dealer. Once you’ve purchased your vehicle, your Ford or Lincoln Mercury dealer can help you coordinate the equipment installation and arrange for the final delivery with your Mobility Modifier. For more information on specific vehicles, please visit www.fordvehicles.com.

Consider discussing these questions with your team:

  • What kind of driving will you be doing – will it be for work or pleasure?
  • Will you be driving from a wheelchair or transferring?
  • Will the needs of your family and friends be met?
  • Will the vehicle accommodate the weight of your conversion, wheelchair and passengers?
  • How will you finance your vehicle?
