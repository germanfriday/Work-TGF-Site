The Mars truck fleet is now being fueled at our filling station. Located at Mars' Stratton Street property, the new filling station has been fitted with an underground 15,000 gallon state-of-the-art diesel fuel storage tank. The new filling station helps us better manage our fuel costs and serve our truck fleet more efficiently.
Mars' new radiation detector upgrades greatly increase our tools to diagnose radioactive material entering the facility. Older radiation detectors simply gave a go/no go alarm to indicate whether or not the car or roll-off box contained radioactive materials. Our new units show on a monitor where in the car or truck the contaminated material is located. Our staff then analyzes the car with hand-held units to more quickly identify and quarantine the materials. The upgraded units add to the number of detectors available for incoming trucks and rail cars.
