As a DuPont AOQ member, you have a business consultant as close as your computer – and you can tap into its services at absolutely no charge.

Free web-based tools from DuPont can help increase profits by auditing your shop’s performance, honing in on areas that need special attention. Assurance of Quality members can access them free!

KPI Tracking Tool
 (Business Analysis Tool)
• Tracks monthly performance of Key Performance Indicators (KPI) of your Collision Repair Center and compares them to industry standards and/or averages of groups in which you participate.

• Compares gross profits, employee efficiencies, and departmental sales as a percent to total sales, average sale per repair order, per estimator and per stall, etc.

Paint Department Optimizersm Tool
• Tracks monthly performance of the paint department.
• Helps manage sales and costs.

Proficient Repair Analysis Tool
• A series of best practice checklists to identify any weak processes in your shop.

º Process checklists include maintenance, safety, body repair, primer, refinish and detail.

Smart Spraybooth Energy Management Tool
• Determines spray booth energy costs per cycle and per year.

º Many factors affect these energy costs. The tool helps provide a better understanding of the factors and how changing them will affect costs.

These valuable tools join features like CSI Reports as another benefit of AOQ membership.

Just visit www.performancecoating.dupont.com, after logging in, click on the DPC Solutions link and then click on Business Management Tools.

If you’re a new user, click on the Register button and enter the required information. Once your information and valid membership is authenticated, you’ll receive a secure user name and password (usually within two business days).
